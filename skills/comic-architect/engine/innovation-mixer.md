# Innovation Mixer (Design Twist)

To ensure variety (365 days of unique outputs), we inject "Micro-Innovations" into the prompt. These are non-destructive modifiers that add flavor without breaking the core content.

## The Formula
`Final Prompt = [Layout] + [Subject] + [Visual Style] + [Rendering Technique] + [Innovation Twist] + [Lighting/Color]`

## 1. Material Twists (Texture)
*Roll 1d10*
1.  **Bioplastic**: Translucent, organic, slightly gooey.
2.  **Frosted Glass**: Blurry background, clean foreground.
3.  **Washi Paper**: Fibrous, textured, soft edges.
4.  **Liquid Metal**: Chrome, melting, reflective.
5.  **Origami Paper**: Folded creases, sharp geometric shadows.
6.  **Velvet/Felt**: Soft, light-absorbing, cozy.
7.  **Neon Tubing**: Physical light tubes forming the shapes.
8.  **Ceramic/Porcelain**: Shiny, smooth, fragile, white with blue patterns.
9.  **Voxel Blocks**: Lego-like construction.
10. **Cardboard Cutout**: DIY aesthetic, rough edges.

## 2. Lighting Modifiers (Atmosphere)
*Roll 1d8*
1.  **Golden Hour**: Warm, low angle, long shadows (Nostalgic).
2.  **Bioluminescence**: Glowing from within, dark environment (Tech/Nature).
3.  **Studio Softbox**: Perfect, even, shadowless (Commercial).
4.  **God Rays (Volumetric)**: Beams of light cutting through dust (Dramatic).
5.  **Neon Noir**: Pink/Blue rim lights, high contrast (Edgy).
6.  **Rembrandt**: Strong single source, triangle of light on cheek (Classic).
7.  **Flat Lighting**: No shadows, purely graphic (2D/Icon).
8.  **Caustics**: Underwater light patterns (Dreamy).

## 3. Color Strategies (Palette)
*Roll 1d6*
1.  **Monochrome**: Single hue + Black/White.
2.  **Pastel/Baby**: Low saturation, high brightness (Cute).
3.  **Neon/Acid**: High saturation, high brightness (Cyber/Rave).
4.  **Earth Tones**: Browns, greens, terracottas (Natural).
5.  **Primary Triad**: Red, Blue, Yellow (Bauhaus/Pop).
6.  **Dopamine**: Rainbow, chaotic, high contrast (Trendy).

## 4. Compositional Twists (Camera)
*Roll 1d5*
1.  **Knolling**: 90-degree overhead flat lay.
2.  **Macro**: Extreme close-up, shallow depth of field.
3.  **Isometric**: 30-degree parallel projection.
4.  **Fish-eye**: Distorted, wide angle, dynamic.
5.  **Cross-Section**: Cut in half, showing the inside.

## Execution
When generating a prompt, if the user hasn't specified these details, **YOU MUST RANDOMLY SELECT** one from each category to ensure the output is unique every time.

**Randomization Technique**:
Since you cannot roll actual dice, use the **current system timestamp** (e.g., the last digit of the current second) or pick an item based on the **number of letters in the user's input** modulo the list size. **Do not always pick the first item.**
