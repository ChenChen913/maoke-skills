# Visual Style Vault (Style DNA)

This vault contains the core aesthetic directions. When a user request is vague ("make it cool"), use the Recommender to select one of these based on the content vibe.

## 1. Minimalist & Clean
- **Notion Style**: Black & white, loose line art, negative space, no shading, occasional purple accent.
- **Swiss International**: Grid-based, sans-serif typography, high contrast, asymmetry, primary colors.
- **Apple Pro**: Sleek, metallic, glassmorphism, soft gradients, high-end tech vibe.
- **Bauhaus**: Geometric shapes, function over form, primary colors (Red, Blue, Yellow), clean lines.

## 2. Artistic & Expressive
- **New Yorker Cartoon**: Pen & ink, ink wash shading, witty, satirical, loose sketch, vermillion accent.
- **Ukiyo-e (Floating World)**: Woodblock print texture, flat perspective, bold outlines, muted natural colors.
- **Pop Art**: Bold colors, halftone dots, comic book style, irony, repetition (Warhol/Lichtenstein).
- **Abstract Expressionism**: Chaos, emotion, splashes, non-representational forms.

## 3. Retro & Nostalgic
- **80s Vaporwave**: Neon pinks/blues, statues, grid landscapes, glitch effects, low-fi VHS.
- **70s Psychedelic**: Swirling patterns, bright colors, fluid shapes, trippy, Art Nouveau influence.
- **50s Atomic Age**: Starbursts, pastel colors, retro-futurism, optimistic technology.
- **Pixel Art**: 8-bit or 16-bit, blocky, limited color palette, video game nostalgia.

## 4. Modern & Trendy
- **Claymorphism (3D Clay)**: Soft, rounded 3D shapes, plasticky texture, cute, friendly, bright colors.
- **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass, transparency, blur, background colors showing through.
- **Memphis Design**: Squiggles, triangles, confetti patterns, clashing colors, flat, playful.
- **Risograph**: Grainy texture, misaligned layers, fluorescent inks, DIY zine aesthetic.

## 5. Narrative & Cinematic
- **Ghibli Style**: Lush backgrounds, detailed clouds, vibrant nature, watercolor feel, nostalgic, peaceful.
- **Wes Anderson**: Symmetrical composition, pastel color palettes, quirky objects, flat lay.
- **Noir**: High contrast black and white, dramatic shadows (chiaroscuro), detective vibes, rain.
- **Cyberpunk**: High tech low life, neon lights, rain, night city, mechanical details, blue and magenta.

## 6. Scientific & Technical
- **Blueprint**: Blue background, white lines, technical measurements, grid paper texture.
- **Botanical Illustration**: Detailed scientific drawing, vintage paper, labeled parts, muted greens/browns.
- **Low Poly**: Geometric 3D, faceted shapes, sharp edges, minimalist digital art.
