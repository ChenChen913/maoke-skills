# 🎨 Visual Style Library

This library defines the aesthetic rendering modes for city visualization.

## 1. Hyper-Realistic (The "Cinematic" Look)
| ID | Name | Keywords |
|----|------|----------|
| `cinematic-realism` | 8K Cinematic Realism | `Hyper-realistic digital illustration`, `8k resolution`, `Unreal Engine 5`, `Ray Tracing`, `Atmospheric perspective`, `Highly detailed textures` |
| `arch-viz` | Architectural Visualization | `ArchDaily style`, `Soft studio lighting`, `Clean lines`, `Modernist composition`, `Neutral color palette`, `Wide angle lens` |

## 2. Artistic & Stylized (The "Creative" Look)
| ID | Name | Keywords |
|----|------|----------|
| `lego-world` | Lego Micro-World | `Lego style`, `Plastic blocks`, `Vibrant primary colors`, `Tilt-shift photography`, `Playful atmosphere`, `Lego minifigures` |
| `paper-craft` | Layered Paper Art | `Multi-layered paper cut`, `Washi paper texture`, `Soft shadows`, `Depth of field`, `Pastel colors`, `Intricate details` |
| `oil-painting` | Impasto Oil | `Thick brush strokes`, `Palette knife texture`, `Van Gogh style`, `Vibrant pigments`, `Expressive movement` |
| `isometric-3d` | Isometric 3D (C4D) | `Octane Render`, `Cinema 4D`, `Pop Mart style`, `Matte ceramic texture`, `Soft occlusion`, `Cute and rounded` |
